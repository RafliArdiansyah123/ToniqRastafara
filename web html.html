Tony q Rastafara

ALBUM

Tony Q started his reggae music career in 1989 with the music group Roots Rock Reggae. Usually performing from cafe to cafe or at music events in Jakarta. After joining many reggae bands such as Exodus and Rastaman, finally in 1994 he formed the music group Rastafara which became quite famous as a proponent of reggae music in Indonesia at that time. Together with Rastafara he released two albums, namely "Dreadlock Hair" (1996) and "Gue Falling In Love" (1997).
